    Why lithium batteries are better than others

    lithium battery

    • Lighter, which reduces the overall weight of the vehicle, allowing it to move faster and more efficiently.
    • Longer service life, on average they work 2-3 times longer
    • Fast charging, can be charged several times faster, sometimes in just 1-3 hours.
    • Safety, do not emit harmful vapors, do not contain acids, have fire or explosion protection systems.
    • Minimal maintenance, no need for electrolyte check or cleaning.
    • Environmental friendliness, less harmful to the environment, as they do not contain lead and acid.

    Lead-acid battery

    • Although lead-acid batteries are cheaper to purchase, they have a short lifespan and require frequent replacement
    • Lead-acid batteries are widespread, but their availability does not compensate for the obsolescence of the technology
    • Charging takes much longer compared to lithium batteries
    • When using lead-acid batteries, you risk inhaling harmful acid fumes
    • Minimal maintenance, no need for electrolyte check or cleaning.
    • Lead-acid batteries quickly lose capacity, especially if they are frequently discharged to zero, which negatively affects their performance and longevity.
