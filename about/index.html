    About Us

    IMECAR is a leader in providing advanced solutions in the electric vehicle industry, focusing on green energy.

    We are a team that offers comprehensive solutions for charging stations, helping to save on infrastructure. Thanks to our approach, you can choose different types of chargers that are ideally suited to your needs.

    Our stations are designed to work in a wide variety of conditions, withstanding temperatures from -20°C to +50°C. We thoroughly test each product to ensure reliability and stable operation regardless of weather conditions.

    Our R&D center is a leader in developing advanced technologies for various transportation solutions. At the same time, our modern production facilities ensure the production of High-Quality Batteries that meet the needs of our Global customers.
